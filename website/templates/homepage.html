AAC

Amateur Astronomy Club

The Amateur Astronomy Club, the oldest and most active at NITK Surathkal, has long nurtured curiosity about the cosmos. Through stargazing nights, science tours, projects, and flagship events like Astrofest, the club makes astronomy engaging and accessible. With three telescopes and four SIGs—Astronomy, Astrophysics, Astronautics, and Astroverse—we offer students hands-on exploration, learning, and discovery under the stars.

Astronomy
Observation

Dedicated to sky-watching, this SIG hosts stargazing, telescope viewings, and night-sky events, bringing students closer to the cosmos.

Astrophysics
Theory

Focused on the science of the universe, it explores black holes, galaxies, particle physics, and cosmology—the “why” behind what we see.

Astronautics
Application

Where engineering meets space, this SIG works on rocketry, satellites, and space-tech projects, offering hands-on innovation beyond Earth.

Media & Publicity
Stories & Imagination

Crafting narratives that inspire, this SIG manages creative content, design, and outreach—spreading ideas through words, visuals, and media to connect people and spark imagination.

Starry Nights

Starry Nights, our signature open-sky event, lets students witness the universe firsthand through guided stargazing, telescope viewings, and interactive discussions—where curiosity meets wonder under the stars.

Astro Committee

The AstroCommittee, one of NITK’s three official fest committees, brings the cosmos to Incident and Engineer. As a part of AAC, we curate shows, exhibits, and telescopic sessions, making space exploration an exciting part of the fest atmosphere.

Sci-Tutor

Our science tours take learning beyond campus, with visits to observatories, research centers, and space facilities. They offer hands-on exposure to cutting-edge science and inspire a deeper connection to the future of space exploration.
